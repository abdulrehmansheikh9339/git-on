About Us

Chase Value is a leading department store retailer in Pakistan, which has been in business since 2009.
It is a one-of-its-kind retail store where all middlemen have been eliminated from the supply chain to minimize the product
cost and transfer all the benefits of the resulting savings to customers in the form of reduced prices.

With a promise of wholesale prices all year round, Chase Value owns four branches in Karachi, one in Multan,
one in Bahawalpur, one in Faisalabad & one in Islamabad, and an e-commerce website that serves all across Pakistan.

Vision:

To be the most preferred, yet affordable fashion and lifestyle brand.

Mission:

To be the most preferred, yet affordable fashion and lifestyle brand.

  • Well-being of the employees and their families.
  • Ease for all external business partners.

One-Stop Shop:

With approximately 8000+ products falling under more than 200 categories, Chase Value offers a one-stop solution for its customers under one roof.
Major categories include fabric, cosmetics, home decor, etc. In the online market,
the business delivers more than 150 orders in all regions of Pakistan.

Fast Delivery Across Pakistan:

Chase Value has divided its logistics operations equally among local riders as well as third-party courier services for fast deliveries.

Six Attributes to Promote E-Commerce in Pakistan:

Though Chase Value kicked off with its brick-and-mortar stores, it aims to promote e-commerce across Pakistan.
